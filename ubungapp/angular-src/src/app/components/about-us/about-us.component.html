Welcome to our About Us/FAQ page here you will find any information that you need about the site.

General questions

Is account registration required?

Account Registration is required if you want to be able to save workouts to your account as well as for reviewing workouts.

How do I add a workout to my account?

To add a workout to your account you need to first be logged in. Then when browsing workouts you can select the green button in the upper right hand corner and it will be added to your account.

Is the website free to use?

Yes the website is completely free, you can create an account and save/create workouts all for free.

Is my data saved forever?

Unless you choose to delete your account our system will save it so there's no reason to worry about losing your data.
